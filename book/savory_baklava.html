name: Savory Baklava description: Ground beef baklava with tzaziki suace. ingredients: - 1 Cup of Chopped Pistachios - 1 Cup of Dried Cranberries - 1/2 of an Onion - 2 Cloves of Garlic - 2 Teaspoons of Salt - 1 Teaspoon of Pepper - 1/2 Teaspoon of Ground Coriander - 1/2 Teaspoon of Cayenne - 1/2 Teaspoon of Nutmeg - 1/2 Teaspoon of Ground Thyme - 1 Tablespoon of Mint - 2 Tablespoons of Sage - 2 Pounds of Ground Beef - 3 Sheets of Puff Pastry - 1 Egg - 4 Cups of Spinach - Vinaigrette Dressing - 2 Cups of Greek Yogurt - 1 Cucumber - 4 Cloves of Garlic - 1/2 of a Lemon - 3 Tablespoons of Mint - 1 Teaspoon of Salt - 1 Teaspoon of Pepper - 1 Teaspoon of Cayenne steps: - Peel and shred cucumber. - Place pinch of salt over cucumber and let sit to drain water. - Combine yogurt, juice from half of a lemon, salt, pepper, cayenne, chopped mint, and minced garlic. - Refrigerate until ready to serve. - Prebake 2 of the sheets of puff pastry for 25 minutes at 375 degrees. - Reserve a small amount of pistachios. - Combine 1/2 of an onion minced, 2 cloves of garlic minced, salt, pepper, coriander, caynne pepper, nutmeg, thyme, mint, chopped sage, and ground beef. - Divide meat into 2 equal portions. - Place a layer of cooked puff pastry covering the bottom of a greased pan. - Evenly spread a layer of ground beef on top of the pastry. - Spread half the pistachios and dried cranberries over the ground beef. - Place another layer of cooked puff pastry on the pistachios and dried cranberries. - Repeat with the remaining beef, pistachios and dried cranberries, and a final layer of uncooked puff pastry. - Combine egg and a teaspoon of water to make an egg wash. - Paint the egg wash on the entire top of the baklava. - Sprinkle the reserved pistachios over the top of the baklava. - Bake for 25 minutes at 375 degrees. - Cover with aluminum foil and bake for another 30 minutes.
